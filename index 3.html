Hi! I'm Jess. It's a pleasure to meet you.

I am a software developer but I'm also much more than that.

My friends would tell you that I'm quiet and caring; compassionate, reflective, and a good listener. I often find myself helping others work through their problems.

In a team I often play the role of mediator. I'm good at communicating effectively with a team, I'm tactful, conscientious, and well organised. I'm a quieter member of the team, reflective & constantly listening and analysing, but quick to speak up to smooth conflict over or explain potential pitfalls - and possible solutions - where they arise.

At University I played the role of team leader in most team projects, usually either at the insistence of the team or due to my nature to plan, communicate, encourage, help and track progress. If you want more insight to how I work within a team, you can view my 123test Team Roles results here.

I'm very passionate about software developing and about learning in general. I enjoy spending my spare time working on personal projects, learning new technologies or methods within currently used technologies. I love working with mathematical algorithms and am especially interested in astrophysics.
